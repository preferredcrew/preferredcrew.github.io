Chef - 45m

Our client is looking for a happy, team player to join their 45m private motor yacht as Chef.  

When aboard, the owners are generally by themselves (2 of them) and their food is described as: Southern (US), best quality ingredients, “comfortable” presentation. There will be times when they entertain guests and then they may ask for the occasional “presentation” of dinner - but nothing over the top. Quality is, of course, top notch.

When the owners join, they will be aboard for the entire summer, but as long term yacht owners, they understand the need for a break for the Chef and will eat off occasionally and take the crew out to dinner at times specifically so the Chef gets a bit of down time. After the longer trips, the owners will generally stay off for up to 4 months at a time. Let’s call it busy with periods of significant down-time. The successful Chef who is moving on from the program has noted that the owner’s like the food they like and are not really interested in being told to try something different…they love good food, but are not “foodies.”

Down time is not completely dead. The crew (8 of them during the guest periods - 3 during down time) are still aboard and the Captain likes to see them fed with good, “happy” food. A bend toward healthy, but no special diets, a good variety (not spaghetti night), and the occasional treat - we all like a little cake now and then!

One of the perks of this program - if you live in the Fort Lauderdale area, MOST of the year, you go home at night. During the summer - not so much, the itinerary is Bermuda and then the Caribbean, but in the winters, very light guest trips and you will mostly go in, cook for the crew (generally 3 as noted) and then home again. If you do not live in the Fort Lauderdale area, of course you can live on the yacht!

The flag is Cayman so they are able to work with any nationality, but US Citizens and Green Card holders will be eligible for other significant perks!

Requirements:

STCW & ENG1 (or other Medical Fit For Duty) - current.

US (preferred) or B1 in hand

Covid Vaccinated (fully)

Non-Smoking, but tattoos (non-offensive) should not be an issue

We do not share wages in either direction - but only bring jobs and people together if we truly believe both sides can be happy!
