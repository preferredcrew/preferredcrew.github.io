Stew - 50m


We are in search of a Steward(ess) to become part of our 50-meter yacht program, primarily based along the US West Coast, extending from Alaska to Mexico/San Diego, with a significant portion of our time spent in Seattle.
Our family embraces a laid-back lifestyle, cherishing nature and the thrill of exploration. While we do host guests a couple of times each month, it's typically just a party of two, occasionally expanding to up to four individuals. It's important to note that our program doesn't revolve around late-night parties. Dinners are served early, and guests retire to their quarters by around 9 PM.
Activities onboard include whale watching, fishing excursions, sightseeing, and more. Although our yacht is undoubtedly high-end, we prioritize wholesome and enjoyable experiences.
The interior team consists of the Chief Stewardess and this position. Both team members share all responsibilities, including service, laundry, and housekeeping. You won't be confined to doing just one specific task.
Our onboard team strikes a perfect balance between spending quality time together and pursuing individual interests. There are ample opportunities for camaraderie, but not so much that you'll grow weary of each other.
We take pride in our remarkably low turnover rate and aim to maintain this trend.
Compensation is competitive, and you'll enjoy 28 days of annual vacation. In addition to this, there are plenty of weekends off and numerous three-day weekends to look forward to.
We require candidates to have at least one year of solid experience, as well as valid STCW and ENG1 certifications. If you are not a US citizen or Green Card holder, we would like to see a valid B1 visa. It's important to note that we occasionally sail into Canada, and candidates with felony convictions, including DUIs, will not be allowed entry into the country and therefore cannot be considered for this role. Smoking and vaping are permitted off the boat, and non-offensive tattoos will not pose any issues.
We are eager to have the Steward(ess) join our team as soon as possible.
