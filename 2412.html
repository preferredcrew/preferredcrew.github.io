Deckhand - 61m

Our client is seeking an experienced Deckhand (about a year on yachts) with a history of making commitments to join their very stable 61m motor yacht. The long term owner knows how to treat crew so that they have exceptionally low turnover. The position is open due to an on-board promotion (they do like to promote from within).

Holding themselves to the highest in yachting standards, they usually run with the owner or his family aboard, but they do select charters as well. They will be leaving Florida before the end of April to get to the Eastern Med for an owner's trip and then off to France for a charter.

The ideal candidate enjoys working with the entire team, is more interested in making things work rather than trying to re-invent the wheel. Traits would be a keen learner, professional but easy going attitude, and if you have some water sports experience, so much the better.

They offer a solid wage, fantastic insurance, and 60 days vacation broken into 2 trips home (call it about 5:1)

What would put a candidate over the top is that learner attitude. They love to train and to hone skills, you will learn how to drive the tender (it is large) as well as other facets of a solidly established program.

Dual season, they run the Mediterranean / Caribbean route.

The existing team is a happy group that works hard, and enjoys a good laugh - the new team member will fit in with that.

  • Candidates must be Male (it is a shared cabin)
  • Have at least the MCA Yacht Rating Certificate in hand (completion of the courses is not enough)
  • Cannot smoke, either on or off the yacht
  • Tattoos under t-shirt / shorts are fine, no other tattoos will be considered
  • They can accept any passport however, candidates who need a Schengen or B1 visa must already have it in hand.
  • Candidates must be vaccinated against Covid

They set sail on the 25th and would like to have the Deckhand in place a good few days before that.
