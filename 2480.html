Chief Stew - 39m 


We are looking for a Chief Stew (English as a Mother Language) to join our classic, 39 meter motor yacht. This pristine classic is a one season (Med based) program looking for a full time, year round Chief. Based in South of France / Monaco, holds up to 8 guests (more during a party) in classic luxury.
The Chief has a Second Stew as her right hand. The Captain is a fantastic, experienced professional who knows how to keep a team on and happy (turnover is very low).
The yacht is used mostly on weekends, but there is a longer boss trip scheduled every August.
Their ideal candidate is not part of a team, has at least a season or two of leadership, knows great service, has a solidly happy disposition and enjoys a good laugh.
Exotic travel is not on the menu here - this is a one season program, so somebody with a life in the area may appreciate the location.
The salary is competitive, vacation is 90 days per year, and they are looking for somebody to start before the Med season (call it April 1).
