Chief Engineer - 43m

Our client is seeking the Engineer candidate to join their team and bring that "work hard - play hard" attitude along. Working with the entire team, you will be focused on brining a happy, yacht perfect, atmosphere to these American owners. 

The yacht is 40m+, new, private (at least until the fall when she starts to Charter), traveling the traditional yachting hotspots from the Caribbean to the Mediterranean.

The family is US, low maintenance (they enjoy going ashore when at the dock to explore the local sites), happy and respectful. They love the boat and the freedom that it offers. Their itinerary is planned out for the next year (light use) and although there is guaranteed to be some unexpected changes, they are serious planners and you will know well in advance where you are going and when guests will be aboard.

They enjoy a very high-end, classical presentation but with a quick smile and a laugh.

It is after reviewing their plans for this year that they see the down time and are opening up to charter after the summer.

Recent longevity is something that will definitely put in the front of the line, since this client is considering only candidates able to make commitments and stick with them through time.

This 43m yacht has that "team" atmosphere - everybody pitches in with every department - the Captain even washes dishes at times. The Engineer will expect to help with tender launching and anchoring as well as pitching in as needed - don't worry, help comes to you when you need it too!

Speaking of help, the yacht is fresh out of the yard. Everything is brand new, iPad and touch screen - they are still under warranty so plenty of shore-side assistance (which you will be expected to use).

So - the requirements:

  • Y3 - they are commercially registered
  • Minimum of 36 solid months of yachting experience you must bring good skills to the table.
  • Fluent English (as a mother tongue is ideal, but fluent will do)
  • Solid longevity - at least year on each boat
  • Of course STCW Basic Training / ENG1 / Solid references (and we do check).
  • B1 and Schengen (at least eligible) visas or Residency that allows you to work in both the Caribbean and the Mediterranean
  • Tattoos are not a problem (non-offensive) but no smoking is permitted on or off the yacht.
  • Join date: last week of May in the Med.
  • The Captain has asked that we share the wages: 5250 Euro and FULL ROTATION - there is no wiggle room here.
  • They do not accept teams on the program.

If you can relate to this, let us know!
