Team Assistant

Join our energetic and dynamic team at a highly esteemed yachting house in beautiful Fort Lauderdale! We are seeking an enthusiastic candidate who is ready to kickstart their career in the yachting industry.
As a Team Assistant, you will have the unique chance to work with various departments, including Yacht Management, Yacht Brokerage, and Charter Brokerage. This role exposes you to all aspects of a well-run yachting house, providing valuable insights and experience.
Your daily tasks may not be glamorous at first, but they are vital to the smooth operation of the office. From answering phones and scheduling appointments to handling mail for both the office team and managed yachts, your contributions are essential. Additionally, you'll be responsible for ensuring the office is well-stocked with supplies, maintaining the coffee machine, and keeping the champagne cooler filled.
We seek someone who exudes genuine cheerfulness, even during busy days, and remains proactive in addressing any needs that arise. Your excellent organizational skills, attention to detail, and hospitality are highly valued in this role, as you'll interact with various individuals, including owners, guests, and service teams.
Requirements:
• Well-organized with a knack for multitasking
• Genuinely cheerful and customer-focused
• Proactive in problem-solving and fulfilling requests
• Exceptional English communication (spoken and written)
• 21 years of age or older (liquor stocking responsibilities)
• Reliable transportation to downtown Fort Lauderdale / 17th Street / nearby yacht yards
• US Legal (Passport or Green Card)
While a college education isn't mandatory, that and fluency in other languages (French, Italian, Spanish) is advantageous. A penchant for natty dressing and an interest in presentation and fashion align well with our yachting culture.
No extensive prior work experience is necessary, but positive references from previous employers or mentors showcasing your work ethic and disposition are required.
We offer competitive wages and attractive benefits, creating a stress-free work environment. If you dream of a fulfilling career in Brokerage, Sales, Management, or Support within the yachting world, this role serves as the perfect jumping-off point.
Apply now and seize this golden opportunity to embark on an exciting journey in the thriving yachting industry!
