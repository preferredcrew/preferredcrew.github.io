Mate - 38m


We are seeking an enthusiastic and skilled Mate to join our team on a 38m Private Motor Yacht, starting in the new year. Currently we are in the yard getting some regular work done (paint, engine works, etc.).
The yacht is owned by an experienced and passionate individual who loves the adventurous lifestyle of constant travel. If you possess any fishing skills, we'd love to hear about them! The yacht traditionally explores both popular destinations and off-the-beaten-path places, providing an excellent opportunity to experience different parts of the world.
Your role as Mate will be vital to the yacht's operations. As part of the Deck team, you will work alongside a capable Deckhand who possesses good skills but may still require some guidance. The ideal candidate for this position should be hands-on and possess strong leadership abilities.
We welcome applications from both men and women, as we do not adhere to traditional gender roles for this opportunity.
To be considered for this position, candidates must hold at least the YM 200 certification, the STCW Basic certification, and a Medical Fit for Duty (ENG1). References will be checked, and both your hands-on abilities and management skills will be thoroughly reviewed.
Equally important to your qualifications is your positive attitude. The Captain is a seasoned yachting professional who values completing work with a smile. We seek someone who genuinely loves being on the water and can share a good laugh with the team.
If you are passionate about yachting and are looking for an exciting opportunity to work with a well-traveled owner and a supportive crew, we encourage you to apply for the Mate position on our Private Motor Yacht.
