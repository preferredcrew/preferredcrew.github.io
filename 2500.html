Marine Technician

Glittering sun. Crystal clear seas. Powder white beaches. Powerful engines, sleek beautiful yachts cutting through paradise. And marine doors.
Not everything in yachting is glittering and pretty. A lot of it is solid hard work, understanding mechanical, electrical, and even some electronics. The doors on yachts are a perfect example.
We are seeking a technician with career drive to join our established team – we consult and troubleshoot those pesky marine doors on yachts. An intricate combination of everything you have learned to date will challenge you into completing a job well done.
Even aboard a single yacht, you will work with multiple types of doors - Electric and manual sliding, pantograph, watertight, locker, and fire doors exist on every yacht out there. Each requires very detailed review, a creative and curious mind to evaluate and troubleshoot, and of course, meticulous follow-through to assure both the performance and beauty of each.
We are happy to train the right candidate – but you must bring the right mind-set to the table. If you have solid experience in air conditioning (mechanical, electrical, electronic), lock smithing, aviation technology, or, of course, yachting you likely have the skills to be very successful with us.
The position we are looking to fill is one that we would like to see step up to lead technician (so you can lead a team of 2 to 3), but this is absolutely a “hands on” position, not a point and delegate spot.
The position is based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida with most of the jobs at boats within an hour or two driving, however some out of town work can be expected. For well-qualified candidates not located in the area, they will consider a relocation package (so if you are in N. Dakota, now is the opportunity to get into the warmth!
The nuts and bolts of the position and the requirements:
Technician Duties and Responsibilities
• Monitor and analyze electrical systems
• Test devices for improvement, safety, and quality control
• Update older electrical systems for changes, cost reductions, improvements, safety, and quality control
• Repair broken equipment and wiring
• Perform and analyze tests to track results and make improvements
• Perform calibrations
• Participating in test runs and meeting safety regulations
• Install new products, and test them to ensure they are working correctly
• Educating the customer on how to use and maintain their device
• Perform maintenance and provide customer support
• Diagnose problems and come up with solutions to repair them
• Ordering parts needed for repairs if needed
• Fill out detailed reports and keep records on services that have been performed
• Explain issues with equipment and repairs to customers
Technician Requirements and Qualifications
• Four to five years of experience as a technician, apprentice technician, or relevant work experience
• Ability to install, repair, service test and troubleshoot equipment
• Able to multitask, prioritize, and manage time efficiently
• Creative problem solver who thrives when presented with a challenge
• Able to analyze problems and strategize for better solutions
• Flexible and able to multitask on several different aspects of a project or on multiple projects
• Able to take initiative to recommend projects and product improvements
• Must be able to meet the physical requirements of the job which include bending, lifting heavy equipment and
• working in confined spaces.
• Technical school degree and certification for technicians
• Proficient computer skills, Microsoft Office Suite (Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Excel)
• Excellent communicator: able to understand instructions and communicate effectively
• Strong mathematical and technical skills
• Driver’s license and reliable transportation required
• Must have own basic hand tools

All candidates who meet the above qualifications will be considered regardless of age, race, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, politics, religion or even eye-color! But your attention to detail, ability to communicate (English), work ethic, personal responsibility, and career focus must all be top notch.
This is a land-based, US position. At this point, only US legal candidates will be considered. Candidates recently out of the military with the requested experience are strongly encouraged to apply. Candidates who are looking to become Tick Tok influencers need not waste their time.
