Interior Manager - 80m+


We are seeking a highly skilled and dedicated Interior Manager (Previously known as Chief Stew) to join our esteemed team on board our 80m+ Charter Motor Yacht. As the Interior Manager, you will play a pivotal role in overseeing various aspects of the interior department, including provisioning, interior HR, organizing parties and special events, as well as planning shipyard projects related to the interior. The ideal candidate possesses strong leadership capabilities, with prior experience as a Chief Steward/Stewardess or leading an Interior team on a Large yacht. 
Position: Interior Manager
Work Structure: Full rotation (the first year, with training and handover it is not quite time for time), working back-to-back with a Senior Interior Manager
Salary: Depending on Experience (DOE)
Benefits: Excellent health insurance
Start Date: October 2023
Key Skills:
Progressive Leadership: Demonstrate a modern leadership style that fosters a culture of inclusivity and values each team member's opinions, encouraging open communication.
Training and Development: Possess a growth mindset and actively nurture an interior culture by providing training, coaching, and opportunities for individual and team growth.
Kindness and Compassion: Display considerate and caring behavior, prioritizing the wellbeing of the team. Recognize the unique work-life balance required on a busy yacht and foster a positive and supportive onboard environment.
Calm and Confident: Stay level-headed in high-pressure situations, maintaining composure, and effective communication. Exude confidence without arrogance.
Collaborative Team Player: Cultivate a collaborative approach, offering assistance to other departments to enhance teamwork and synergy.
Innovative Problem Solver: Exhibit excellent problem-solving skills, even under pressure, while encouraging creative thinking to address challenges effectively.
Pre-requisites/Qualifications:
Minimum of 7 years in the yachting industry.
At least 2 years of experience in a Senior Interior position.
Proficiency in Microsoft Office.
B1 B2 Visa would be beneficial.
WSET Level 2 or above certification.
Extensive knowledge of cocktails and spirits.
Level 2 Food and Hygiene certification.
If you are a dynamic individual with the necessary experience and skills, and you're seeking an exciting opportunity to lead and thrive in a luxury yacht environment, we invite you to apply.
