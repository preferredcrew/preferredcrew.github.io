Stewardess - 47m


We are in search of a Stewardess with at least 2 years of proven experience to join our private 47-meter motor yacht.
For the first time in quite a while, we have a crew member departing to pursue a land-based opportunity. We are now looking for that exceptional individual—a highly qualified, cheerful team player eager to make a long-term commitment.
During the winter, our yacht sees frequent use as the owner embarks on trips between Florida and the Bahamas. For the remainder of the year, we call South Florida our home berth. While our program may not be the most adventurous, it is undeniably refined and civilized.
Our owner is a seasoned yacht owner who treats the crew with respect and consideration, likely a key factor in our remarkably low turnover rate. Our yacht is designed to accommodate Stewardesses comfortably, with a minimalist approach that avoids excessive glass or shiny surfaces.
The Stewardess will serve as the second member of our interior team, collaborating closely with the Chief Stewardess in various capacities, including service, cabin upkeep, and laundry. The Chief Stewardess is hands-on, and so will you be.
To be considered for this position, candidates must meet the following criteria:
• Possess a US Passport, Green Card, or a valid B1 visa.
• Reside in Florida (living in the local area is preferred, as it allows you to return home most nights when not on a trip).
• Hold a current STCW Basic Training certification and an ENG1 (or equivalent fit-for-duty medical certificate).
• Have at least 2 solid years of experience in the yachting industry (with a slight wiggle room of 1-2 months maximum).
• Demonstrate a track record of longevity in previous roles.
• Be comfortable with a thorough background check and drug test (we have a zero-tolerance policy).
• Tattoos are acceptable, provided they are not offensive and are not located on your face or neck.
• Smoking is strictly prohibited.
We have a team-friendly program, and if your partner happens to be an experienced Assistant Engineer we can consider him/her as well.
