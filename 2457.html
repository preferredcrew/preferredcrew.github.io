Bosun - 45m


Are you a professional Bosun who happens to love diving and water sports? I mean LOVE diving and water sports? Our client has an opportunity for a Bosun to join their 45m very private motor yacht. You will be leading the team on deck, you will be standing a proper watch, you will be exactly what you would expect as a Bosun...and your side passion of diving and water sports will be used pretty much whenever the guests are aboard.
The program is quite calm - the owner and family are happy, respectful, active people, but they have land lives as well as yachting lives. When they come to the boat - you are an integral part of the program.  
By the way - if you can fish - that's a plus too!
The requirements to be considered:
(1) STCW and ENG1 - valid and in hand
(2) Able to pass a pretty thorough background check - this includes drug testing and even weed will not pass.
(3) Some kind of advanced Dive certification, Instructor, Rescue, etc.
(4) Good tender driving skills
(5) Great attitude and team player
(6) History of making commitments (the person who is leaving has been there well over 3 years).
The boat lives in South Florida and the Bahamas so there is a real plus if you have either a US Passport or a Bahamian Passport.
The position starts in December, so candidates who are currently employed and would have to give proper notice can be considered.
