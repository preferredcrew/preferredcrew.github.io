Location: Holland (initial departure to Florida / Panama Canal on September 12)

About the Yacht:

Length: 47m
Year: 2020
Builder: Delta
Program: Private

Fully vaccinated, boosted, and drug-free
Possession of STCW2010 and ENG-1 (or USCG Maritime Physical)
US B1B2 Visa
Proven longevity in previous yacht programs
Clear drug screening
Non-smoking/vaping

Total Crew: 8 members

Regular 8-day trips with 2 to 6 guests

If you are passionate about creating outstanding culinary experiences and thrive in an environment that values teamwork and exploration, we look forward to receiving your application.
