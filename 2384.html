First Mate - 45m

Are you looking for a great spot that allows about 8 months of having a personal life…and 4 months of working as hard as you ever have? Are you passionate about the US East Coast? Do you love the sun and salt-life that defines Florida and the Bahamas? We have the Mate position for you!

This 45m custom built motor yacht was designed, built and operated for the pride and enjoyment of the owners. They spend most of the fall/winter/spring in and around Fort Lauderdale - which means if you have a family, you can plan on being home a good number of nights (yep - it is one of those unicorn jobs). In the summer, that is out the door - the owner moves aboard and spends a good 3 months cruising up and down the US East Coast, enjoying life, getting on his fishing boat, living the salt life! 

This is not a stuffy program - even when guests are aboard. No shoulder boards required. The yacht is immaculate - the crew is well presented, but the formal whites - just not part of this program.

The opportunity is ideal for either an established First Mate who has found that 150’ range to be the perfect size for them - a mix of leadership and hands on. Or for that very experienced Second Mate / Bosun who is looking to step up into the First Mate role.

The yacht is beautifully maintained, with proper planning and maintenance, she is a dream to run. The deck team is a proper team, one for all and all for one to bring out the very best that yachting has to offer. As a leader, they want you to be even-keeled (sorry for the pun), lead by example, and respect your team! Ideally they want you to impart your knowledge to the rest of your team, teaching, guiding and generally working as a hands-on mentor. Your opinions on improvements are listened to!

The cabin is shared so they are only able to consider male candidates, but if you have 4 years of yachting experience, this could be the step that propels you in the right direction! Turn over is very, very low with the Captain, Chief Engineer and Chief Stew very long term crew.

If this spot is so good, why is it open? Simple - the previous Mate has moved to a program with his partner for the next step in his career - unfortunately for him, this is just not a program that can accommodate a team.

They require the Chief Mate 3,000 Ticket (or CeC) or better
Four years of yachting experience
US Green Card or US Passport

If you are ready to work hard, and you love the ocean, and want to build your personal life in South Florida, don’t let this opportunity pass you by!
