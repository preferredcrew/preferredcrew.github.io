Chief Stew - 38m


Are you looking for that perfect wet-foot / dry-foot yachting position? That spot that combines the hard work, travel, and teamwork of yachting with the Monday - Friday hours of a "regular" job? We've got it!
Our client is looking for a US-legal Chief Stew to join their Miami based program. When the owners are not on, you will be working Monday to Friday (that is right - weekends off)! When they are using the yacht, the trips generally last 3 weeks and are in and around the Bahamas (so if you have a cat at home, you will need somebody who is happy to feed it).

This is the perfect job for that experienced Chief who is looking for more of a work-life balance in South Florida but still loves the sea, the perks of yachting and great service.
The position is open now and they would like to have the spot filled before July 10.
All of our usual requirements hold - STCW Basic, Medical Fit for Duty, Great References.
