Stewardess - 50m


Join our prestigious team as a Stewardess on our luxurious 50m private motor yacht, offering a unique and rewarding experience unlike any other. Embrace a tranquil pace that stands apart from the hectic norms of yachting, prioritizing meticulous standards, a harmonious team spirit, and enduring commitment.
As a member of our intimate 3-person interior team, consisting of the Chief Stewardess and two additional Stewardesses, you'll indulge in the charm of alternating seasons between the picturesque landscapes of New England summers and the inviting allure of South Florida, occasionally venturing to the enchanting Bahamas.
Uniqueness is the hallmark of this role. We're seeking a candidate with a penchant for detail, potentially exhibiting a touch of the "OCD" trait. While formal occasions showcase your prowess in elegant plated service alongside your colleagues, daily responsibilities primarily revolve around housekeeping and laundry duties, requiring an unparalleled attention to precision, particularly noted by our discerning Madam.
Our esteemed owners, a delightful and respectful elderly couple, boast an impressive track record of fostering enduring relationships with their dedicated crew members and household staff, exemplifying remarkable longevity in commitment.
The ideal candidate for this role transcends the conventional stewardess profile. Possessing a mature demeanor, complemented by a background in yachting, previous service and housekeeping expertise, and a history of steadfast commitments, you are primed to excel in this distinctive opportunity.
Please note that due to shared accommodation arrangements, only applications from female candidates will be considered for this position. While holding the essential STCW Basic Training and ENG1 certifications is required, an additional preference is granted to candidates already possessing the B1 visa (though exceptions can be made for outstanding candidates). Regardless of your current location worldwide, your application will be welcomed and thoughtfully evaluated.
Enjoy a highly competitive remuneration package, along with a month-long vacation period and comprehensive medical insurance coverage, all adding to the allure of this exceptional role.
If you're ready to embark on a journey that celebrates your attention to detail, your commitment to excellence, and your dedication to enduring relationships, we invite you to apply and become an integral part of our distinguished yachting family.
