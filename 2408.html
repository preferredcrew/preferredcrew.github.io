Captain - 50m


Our client is looking for a rotational Captain to join their 50m world-travelling motor program.  The program is completely private (zero charters ever) and the owner describes himself as "difficult."
There is no management company so the Captains must maintain the paperwork and accounts aboard - although the information is all run through the owner's company (land based) for veractiy and consistencey.  

Crew turnover has been a challenge as the owner frequently changes his mind and the program will run east in the morning, and west in the evening - it is that much of a constant change.  The Engine room has been very stable, but Interior and Galley are always in a state of flux.
The owner is not live-aboard but he is as close to it as you can be.  He understands the stress this puts on the Captain and therefore this is a full rotation spot.

This is not a position for everybody.  If you are able to handle a significant amount of chaos (our words, not his), are excited by a circumnavigation, and find the rewards of rotation worth the challenge - let us know.

Candidates will only be considered if they meet the following minimum requirements (there are no exceptions)
1. Master 3,000 or greater acceptable to the MCA
2. STCW Basic / ENG1 valid and in hand
3. Verifiable references that attest to your flexibility and ability to deal with difficult people
4. History of making permanent commitments
5. No team candidates will be considered
6. No smoking, no vaping, no chewing tobacco of any sort

On a side note from Preferred - this is not a program for everybody.  If what truly matters to you is extensive world-wide travel and rotation, this may work well for you (the other Captain has been with him for a significant period of time), but if you are looking for a position that is "friend/confidant of the owner" - this is not for you.
