Purser / Financial - 85m


Start Date: September 2023
Are you a highly competent and dedicated Financial Officer looking for an exciting opportunity to join a prestigious team on board a luxurious Motor Yacht? If so, we have the perfect position for you!
Position Overview: We are seeking a talented and experienced Financial Officer to join our onboard team. As the Rotational Financial Officer, you will play a crucial role in overseeing various processes, including accounts, logistics, crew and guest support, administration, and providing support to other Heads of Department, the Executive Officer, and the Captain. This position was previously the Purser role and encompasses many of these duties, with a heavy focus on the financial management of the vessel.
Responsibilities:
• Manage organizational operations onboard, including accounts, logistics, crew and guest support, and administration.
• Collaborate with other Heads of Department, the Executive Officer, and the Captain to provide comprehensive financial support and ensure smooth operations.
• Ensure accurate and timely financial reporting, maintaining meticulous attention to detail in all data entry processes.
• Foster a calm and compassionate working environment, demonstrating fairness and empathy.
• Actively listen and respond to feedback, adapting SOPs as needed.
• Remain proactive in identifying areas for improvement and implementing solutions to enhance vessel performance.
• Demonstrate excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to think creatively and adapt to evolving situations.
• Exhibit effective communication and interpersonal skills to collaborate with diverse teams and stakeholders.
• Uphold a high level of integrity, ethics, and confidentiality.
Qualifications:
• Minimum 3 years of experience in the yachting industry, with a solid understanding of yacht operations and financial management.
• 1-2 years of experience in an administration role, preferably within the yachting or hospitality industry.
• Financial qualifications or relevant certifications are preferred.
• Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook), with a high level of competency in Excel.
Benefits: In return for your expertise and dedication, we offer a competitive salary package and an opportunity to be part of a dynamic and prestigious yacht team. You'll have the chance to work in a breathtaking environment and contribute to the seamless functioning of an iconic motor yacht.
If you possess the required qualifications and believe you are a suitable candidate for this role, we encourage you to apply. Join us on board and embark on an extraordinary journey where your financial acumen and operational skills will shine. Apply now and be a part of the finest team in the yachting industry.
