Chief Mate 41m

We are currently seeking a Chief Mate to join a 41+m expedition style motor yacht where the sea miles rack up fast. The Chief, the Captain and the Deckhand are the entire Deck team so you know this is “all hands on deck.” This is not a paperwork position – you are not just willing to get out and work with the rest of the Deck team – you are excited to do so.
The Captain wants a candidate perfectly comfortable moving the very large tender with (or without) guests a couple of hundred nautical miles without needing your hand held. Safety is critical!
The position requires proper seamanship: standing watch, chart reading, docking, launching, and overall boat handling skills are required. They train you on this particular yacht, but your references need to clearly indicate your seamanship skills are Chief Mate quality.
Fishing –an avid fisherman who really enjoys the sea-going lifestyle!
The yacht’s personality is “old school” and “gentlemanly” – in other words, we always refer to the owners as Mister and Missus, we offer our hands to assist them on and off. We greet them with an honest smile and genuine concern. Not only is this a truly seamanship heavy program – it is yachting, and that means the highest level of service.
The rest of the team is well established and runs smoothly. Everybody gets on well and pitches in where needed.
Due to the very active nature of the program, candidates with a strong sense of healthy, active lifestyles will likely fit in better than crew looking to hit the next bar.
The cabin is shared, but they have flexibility and are happy to consider male, female or genderX candidates.
In addition to the skills and abilities already noted, the Chief should be mature enough to handle responsibilities and active enough to live up to the physical needs of the position.
Candidates needing sea-time to move to the next level are STRONGLY encouraged to apply.  
At this point, the OOW is required for consideration.
A history of making commitments is required.
They yacht is positively non-smoking, but small, discreet tattoos are not an issue – no neck, face, sleeve, or similar styles will be considered. Piercings (other than ears) are also out.
They offer 60 days vacation the first year – with a possibility of rotation after that. 
They would like to have the new Chief aboard by Mid November and embarkation will be in the US (Florida). Candidate from anywhere in the world will be considered as long as you have, or can get, the B1/B2 visa.
Currently the plan is Pacific adventure cruising up and down the Americas (Middle and North) West Coast.
If you are interested in a traditional, moving, adventure yacht – let us know.
