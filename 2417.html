Chief Stew - 40m+

Our client is seeking Chief Stewardess candidate to join their team and bring that "work hard - play hard" attitude along. Leading (not just point and scream – but mentoring) a team of two other Stews, you will be focused on brining a happy, yacht perfect, atmosphere to these American owners. So, you will be doing, learning and teaching - the trifecta of a career position!

The yacht is 40m+, new, private (at least until the fall), traveling the traditional yachting hotspots from the Caribbean to the Mediterranean.

The family is US, low maintenance (they enjoy going ashore when at the dock to explore the local sites), happy and respectful. They love the boat and the freedom that it offers. Their itinerary is planned out for the next year (light use) and although there is guaranteed to be some unexpected changes, they are serious planners and you will know well in advance where you are going and when guests will be aboard.

They enjoy a very high-end, classical presentation but with a quick smile and a laugh.

One of the challenges is that the family is so warm and welcoming, the previous Chief has told us that it can be difficult to “maintain the line” between guests and crew – so the most successful Chief candidate will likely have been a Chief previously.

It is after reviewing their plans for this year that they see the down time and are considering opening up to charter after the summer.

Recent longevity is something that will definitely put in the front of the line, since this client is considering only candidates able to make commitments and stick with them through time.

Speaking of requirements…here they are:

  • Minimum of 36 solid months of yachting experience - this is a proper Chief position and you must bring good skills to the table.
  • Fluent English (as a mother tongue is ideal, but fluent will do)
  • Solid longevity - at least year on each boat
  • Of course STCW Basic Training / ENG1 / Solid references (and we do check).
  • B1 and Schengen (at least eligible) visas or Residency that allows you to work in both the Caribbean and the Mediterranean
  • Join date – 30-45 days 
  • All of our basic requirements on the requirements page

The wages are good, but we don’t share them - that is for the two sides to agree on after meeting.  Vacation - 42 paid days off per year.

They do not accept teams on the program.

If you can relate to this, let us know!
