Second Stewardess - 46m


We have a fantastic opportunity for a unique team!
Are you a well-rounded and experienced Stew, ready to join a dynamic team and a carefully planned program that allows you to see the world?
This adventure yacht is looking for an experienced Stew with at least 2 years of experience in the industry, able to follow clear, direct orders, while cherishing team spirit. You will work hand in glove with the Chief Stew to give these owners the fun-loving experience they so crave. Every detail is seen too, every trip is an adventure. You are professional, and love both perfection and a laugh.
The itinerary is both comfortable and extensive. Currently based in the Pacific (near the Americas), they are planning an adventure that is off the beaten path – cruising where few yachts go (which is why your sense of adventure is important).
The owners and Captain are very crew centric. There is a budget for constant training and the watchword is “improvement.”
Although the owners do not overuse the boat, when they are aboard, it is all hands-on deck! Toys in the water, tendering them around, service, entertainment, basically the yacht is their vacation, and they love it.
The Captain runs a very tight ship and the structure is constant. Their expectations are outlined right from the beginning and everybody from Junior to Captain is held to the same standards. The program is busy - you will not have time to check your cell phone throughout the day.

STCW Basic Training
ENG1

Non-smokers Non-Vape only.

Not rotation - they have 38 vacation days the first year.
The wages are quite good - but will be based on the experience and skills you bring to the table.
The Deckhand position (the other side of the team) is JUST as important!
Are you a seriously “hands-on” Deckhand? Do you have a sense of being yacht-proud where you understand that the only job that is done is one that is done right? Do you crave the kind of program that goes way off the beaten path? If this is you - we have something that may seriously interest you.
Our client is looking for an experienced (minimum 1 year) Deckhand to join their 45m private motor yacht. This yacht was launched recently, is 100% private, owned by a family that not only says “please” and “thank you,” but that is described as “simply wonderful, down to earth owners.”
A totally private yacht, they do not require a license, but a Deckhand with a career path will certainly stand out in the crowd.
