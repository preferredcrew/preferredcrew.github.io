Chief Stew - 50m


We are looking for a professional, mature, Chief Stewardess to join our 50m Private Motor Yacht.
As Chief, you will be responsible for running the interior department of the yacht, working closely with the Owner who is actively involved in all aspects of the interior. You will lead a team of two interior crew members and maintain the highest standards of service and maintenance.
Key Responsibilities:
• Interior Maintenance: Ensure the daily upkeep of the interior and oversee weekly and monthly duties.
• Provisioning: Handle all guest and crew provisioning, uniform purchasing, and miscellaneous interior items, including wine purchasing.
• Financial Accountability: Manage monthly interior accounts and conduct full interior inventories twice a year for management purposes.
• Collaboration: Work closely with the Engineer to ensure regular maintenance of the Interior and ensure the AV system is functioning optimally.
• Yard Periods: Ensure all necessary interior maintenance work is completed during yard periods and coordinate with contractors as needed.
• Owner Interaction: Serve as the primary point of contact for the Owners when they are onboard, responsible for all service, while other interior crew members work behind the scenes. Additionally, be the point of contact when the Owners are not on board.
• Guest Services: Collaborate with the Captain on guest itineraries, organize activities, and manage restaurant reservations, liaising with agents when necessary.
• Culinary Collaboration: Work closely with the Head Chef to maintain accurate preference sheets and records for the benefit of the guests.
Qualifications:
• Proven experience as a Chief Stewardess on a luxury yacht or in a similar role.
• Strong leadership and team management skills.
• Exceptional attention to detail and commitment to maintaining high standards.
• Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
• Ability to work effectively with owners, guests, and crew.
• Proficiency in yacht interior maintenance and service protocols.
• Knowledge of provisioning and inventory management.
• Strong organizational and problem-solving skills.
• Current on ENG1 and STCW Basic Training.
• English spoken at a “native” level.
Benefits:
• Up to 90 days of vacation per year.
• Competitive pay package.
