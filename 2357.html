Technical Manager

Our client has an opening for a Technical Manager in their yacht management fleet.  

This position works hand in hand with the Technical Director to offer shore-side support for the various yachts in the fleet. 

A clear understanding of the technical systems on yachts, firm grasp of the ISM, ISPS, SOPEP, etc. and the ability to work within these structures will help define the ideal candidate. The ability to communicate clearly with the onboard team - Captain, Chief Engineer and Chief Stew to assist in arranging yard work-lists, project management, etc. is what will make you successful in the role.

You will have solid experience on yachts (ship-only experience will not be enough). Preferably in a position of leadership (First Mate / First Engineer) where you were not only hands on, but also responsible for the outcome. Long term commitments on yachts, through both operational and yard work (regular and exceptional).

You still have a "learning" mindset, a team-attitude, and a genuinely happy outlook on life (every yacht project has issues - if you get mired in the negative, this job is not for you).

The position is based out of West Palm Beach Florida and is currently open, but they are happy to wait for the right candidate.

To be considered for the position candidates must:

Be a US Citizen or have a Green Card (no exceptions)

Have solid yachting experience & a history of making commitments

Be available to travel internationally 

Have solid experience with the onboard systems and the ISM / ISPS

Be very organized.

Be flexible.
